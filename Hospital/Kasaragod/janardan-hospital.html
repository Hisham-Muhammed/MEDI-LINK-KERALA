About the Hospital

Founded in 1963 by Dr. K.P. Janardan, this is one of the oldest and most trusted private multi-specialty hospitals in Kasaragod. Centrally located near the KSRTC Bus Station, it has been a pioneer in medical excellence for over 60 years.
